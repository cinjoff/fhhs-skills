# Fundraising Basics

## Round Expectations
| Round | Amount | What You Need |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| Pre-seed | $250K-$1M | Team + idea + early validation |
| Seed | $1M-$4M | Product + users + retention |
| Series A | $8M-$20M | Revenue + growth + unit economics |

## Process
1. **Prepare** (2-4 weeks): Deck, data room, target list, practice pitch
2. **Warm intros** (ongoing): Never cold email — get introduced
3. **First meetings** (2-3 weeks): Tell your story, gauge interest
4. **Deep dives** (1-2 weeks): Due diligence with interested firms
5. **Term sheets** (1 week): Negotiate terms, not just valuation
6. **Close** (2-4 weeks): Legal, wire, celebrate briefly, get back to work

## Key Terms
- **SAFE:** Simple Agreement for Future Equity — standard for pre-seed/seed
- **Valuation cap:** Maximum valuation your SAFE converts at
- **Discount:** Percentage discount on next round price
- **Pro-rata:** Right to invest in future rounds (VCs care about this)

## Investor Meeting Tips
- Lead with the problem and market, not your solution
- Show traction/validation data (even small numbers)
- Be honest about risks — sophisticated investors respect this
- Ask for advice, not money (paradoxically more effective)
- Follow up within 24 hours with anything promised
