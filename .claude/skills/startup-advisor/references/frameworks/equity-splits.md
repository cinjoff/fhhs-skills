# Equity & Ownership

## Co-Founder Splits
- **Equal split** works when: equal commitment, complementary skills, starting together
- **Unequal split** works when: one founder started earlier, significantly different contributions, one is part-time
- Always use **4-year vesting, 1-year cliff** — protects everyone

## Employee Equity
| Hire # | Typical Range |
|--------|--------------|
| 1-2 | 1-2% |
| 3-5 | 0.5-1% |
| 6-10 | 0.25-0.5% |
| 11-20 | 0.1-0.25% |

All with 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff.

## Advisor Equity
- Standard: 0.25-1% over 2 years
- Must provide real, ongoing value (not just a name)
- Monthly check-in minimum

## Common Mistakes
- Giving equity for ideas (ideas are worth nothing)
- No vesting (someone leaves day 2 with 30% of the company)
- Giving too much to advisors who don't deliver
- Not having a buyback clause for departing founders
