# Growth Channels

## Channel Selection Framework
Pick 1-2 channels. Master them before adding more.

### By Stage
- **Pre-launch:** Content marketing, community building, waitlist
- **0-100 users:** Direct outreach, communities, partnerships
- **100-1000:** SEO, content, referrals, targeted ads
- **1000+:** Paid acquisition, partnerships, PR, product-led growth

### By Model
| Business Type | Primary Channels |
|---------------|-----------------|
| B2B SaaS | Content + SEO, LinkedIn outreach, partnerships |
| Consumer app | Social media, referral loops, community |
| Marketplace | Supply-side first (cold outreach), then demand (SEO/ads) |
| Developer tools | Open source, docs/content, community, integrations |
| E-commerce | Paid social, SEO, influencer, email |

## Channel Evaluation
For each potential channel, score:
1. **Reach** — Can you reach enough of your target users?
2. **Cost** — What's the CAC through this channel?
3. **Speed** — How fast can you get results?
4. **Scalability** — Does it get better or worse at scale?
5. **Defensibility** — Can competitors easily copy this?

## First 100 Customers
Almost always requires "doing things that don't scale":
- Personal emails and DMs (not mass blasts)
- Showing up where your customers hang out
- Offering to set up the product for them
- White-glove onboarding
- Manual referral asks
