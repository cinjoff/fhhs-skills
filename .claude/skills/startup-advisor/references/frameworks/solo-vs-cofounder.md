# Solo vs Co-Founder

## Decision Framework

### Solo Founder Works When
- Technical founder building a tool for their own use case
- Consulting/agency model (services, not product)
- Bootstrapped lifestyle business
- You've built and shipped products before

### Co-Founder Essential When
- Fundraising (VCs strongly prefer teams)
- Two distinct skill sets needed (technical + sales, for example)
- High-complexity product requiring parallel workstreams
- You need accountability and emotional support for the journey

### Choosing a Co-Founder
- **Work together first** — 2-4 week project before committing
- **Complementary skills** — not duplicate skills
- **Aligned on ambition** — lifestyle business vs. unicorn is a fundamental split
- **Conflict resolution** — how do you disagree? Test this early
- **Vesting** — always 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff, even among friends

### Common Mistakes
- Giving equity to idea people who won't execute
- 50/50 splits without discussing decision-making authority
- Skipping the vesting conversation
- Choosing friends over competence
