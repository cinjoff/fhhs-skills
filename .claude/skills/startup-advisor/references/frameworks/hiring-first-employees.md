# Hiring First Employees

## When to Hire
- You've validated PMF or are very close
- You personally can't handle the workload
- Specific skill gap that's blocking growth
- NOT: "we raised money, time to hire" (common mistake)

## First Hires by Stage
1. **Pre-PMF:** Only co-founders + maybe 1 contractor
2. **Early PMF:** First engineer, first sales/support (depending on model)
3. **Growing:** Hire to remove founder bottlenecks, one role at a time

## How to Hire
- **Referrals first** — your network and investors' networks
- **Work together before committing** — paid trial project (1-2 weeks)
- **Optimize for generalists** — specialists come later
- **Equity:** Early employees: 0.5%-2%, with 4-year vesting

## Red Flags
- "I want to work at a startup for the experience" (tourists)
- Can't work without detailed specifications
- Overqualified and likely to leave when things get hard
- Only interested in equity upside, not the mission
