<objective>
Add named milestones as a first-class workflow concept. Currently, work flows as an
endless stream of phases with no natural grouping or archiving point. Milestones provide:
(1) topic-based grouping ("v3.0 Ecosystem & Sync"), (2) natural archiving points for
completed phases, (3) scope boundaries for auto execution, (4) better mental model for
planning — "does this new phase belong in this milestone or start a new one?"

Milestones have a version AND a short name. The name helps decide scope: "This is pipeline
work — it belongs in the Pipeline milestone, not the Ecosystem one."
</objective>
